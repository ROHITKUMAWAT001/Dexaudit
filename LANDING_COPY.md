## 3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: What exactly is DexAudit?**
> A: DexAudit is a specialized financial calculator for engineering teams. It analyzes your current subscriptions to AI tools (like Cursor, Claude, and Copilot) against your actual team size to detect ghost seats, tier mismatches, and overall capital waste.

**Q: How does the AI audit work technically?**
> A: You input your stack, and our deterministic engine cross-references it against real-time vendor pricing and minimum seat thresholds. If you are overpaying, we use an LLM to generate a personalized, boardroom-ready report explaining exactly how to downgrade or consolidate for maximum savings.

**Q: Is my data secure and private?**
> A: 100% Yes. We operate on a zero-retention policy for your proprietary stack data. We do not require OAuth access to your AWS or GitHub environments, and we never use your data to train external models.

**Q: How accurate are the generated insights?**
> A: Our engine maintains a 98% confidence interval. Because we rely on deterministic math (e.g., catching a 5-seat minimum on Claude Team when you only have 2 engineers) rather than AI guesswork, the financial insights are highly defensible and ready for CFO review.

**Q: How much time does it take to see value?**
> A: Less than 3 minutes. You don't need to create an account or connect APIs. Just select your tools, input your team size, and the engine generates your savings report instantly.
