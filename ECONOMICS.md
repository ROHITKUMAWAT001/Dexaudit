## 1. The Revenue Model

DexAudit operates on a hybrid Freemium/SaaS model, designed to act as a self-sustaining lead generation engine for Credex's core secondary market business, while generating its own independent revenue.

### Pricing Tiers
1. **Free Tier ($0):** Single-tool audit. Drives viral growth via public share links (watermarked with DexAudit).
2. **Pro Tier ($49/audit or $490/yr):** The bread and butter. Multi-tool stack audit, ghost seat detection, and API access. Target: Series A startups.
3. **Enterprise (Custom, ~$2,000/yr):** For Series C+. Includes manual auditor review, SLA, and quarterly ROI reviews.

## 2. Unit Economics (Per Lead)

What is a converted lead worth to Credex?

*Assumption: A mid-sized engineering team (50 devs) spends roughly $30,000/yr on AI tools.*

*   **Savings Identified by DexAudit:** Average 20% ($6,000/yr).
*   **Credex Secondary Market Margin:** If Credex fulfills those optimized credits and takes a 15% cut on the transaction volume.
*   **Estimated Lead Value:** For every startup that completes an audit and books a consultation, the lifetime value to Credex's primary business is roughly **$4,500**.

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
Based on our zero-budget GTM strategy (Hacker News, SEO, Viral Loops):
- **Organic CAC:** **$0** (Excluding founder time/salary).
- **Blended CAC Target (18 months):** **<$150**. Since we rely on a free utility tool (the "Mint for AI"), our acquisition cost will remain structurally lower than traditional paid-ads B2B SaaS.

## 3. Conversion Funnel & Profitability

To understand profitability, we must map the conversion from a cold website visitor to a paying Credex customer.

| Stage | Metric / Action | Expected Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Top of Funnel** | Lands on dexaudit.com | 100% |
| **Activation** | Runs a Free Audit (No Login) | 40% |
| **Lead Capture** | Unlocks Full Report (Email Gate) | 15% |
| **SaaS Revenue** | Upgrades to Pro Plan ($49) | 3% |
| **Credex Upsell** | Books Credex Consultation | 1.5% |

**The Math:** For every 1,000 visitors:
- 400 run audits.
- 60 provide emails.
- 12 buy the Pro Plan ($588 upfront cash flow).
- 6 book consultations (Estimated $27,000 pipeline value for Credex).

This funnel makes the standalone DexAudit tool profitable on day one, while functioning as a massive pipeline generator.

## 4. The Path to $1M ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)

To achieve $1,000,000 in ARR strictly from DexAudit SaaS subscriptions (ignoring the massive upside for Credex's core business), we need a specific volume of users.

### The Required Metrics
Assuming a blended ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) of $500/year (mostly Pro plans, sprinkled with a few Enterprise deals):

**Target Customers Needed = 2,000 Active Paying Workspaces.**

### The 18-Month Timeline

| Phase | Timeframe | Goal | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1: Validation** | Months 1-3 | $50k ARR (100 users) | "Show HN", Twitter outbound, targeting Seed-stage founders. Focus on product-led growth (watermarked PDFs). |
| **Phase 2: Distribution** | Months 4-9 | $300k ARR (600 users) | Partner with VC firms. Offer DexAudit as a free "due diligence" tool for their portfolios. Start producing content on engineering efficiency. |
| **Phase 3: Scale** | Months 10-18 | $1M ARR (2,000 users) | Launch the API. Integrate directly into Rippling/Ramp so audits happen automatically when a corporate card is charged for an AI tool. |

### What must be true to hit this?
1. **The Overspend Hypothesis is Real:** Teams must actually be wasting money. If everyone is perfectly optimized, the tool provides no value. (Data suggests this is a very safe bet).
2. **Frictionless Onboarding:** The "no-login" architecture must remain intact to preserve the 40% activation rate.
3. **The Viral Loop Holds:** The PDF exports and share links must act as a trojan horse, spreading the tool from one CTO to another within private networks.
